Heritage Meets Refreshment

Imagine a summer afternoon, a gentle breeze, the clink of ice in a glass, and the unmistakable fragrance of Rooh Afza filling the air. Crafted with nature's botanical treasures, this ruby-red syrup has stood the test of time bridging old and new, heritage and innovation, flavor and feeling. It's not just a drink, it's a memory you can taste. Rooh Afza is an iconic South Asian beverage concentrate and syrup with a rich heritage dating back to 1906. Created by Hakim Hafiz Abdul Majeed in Old Delhi, India, it has become a cultural icon across India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, and the Middle East.

CLASSIC ROOH AFZA SHARBAT

Prepation

  1. Pour Rooh Afza syrup into a glass
  2. Add cold water and stir well
  3. Add ice cubes
  4. Garnish with mint leaves and lemon slice
  5. Serve immediately

ROOH AFZA MILK

Prepation

  1. Mix Rooh Afza syrup with cold milk
  2. Stir until completely blended
  3. Add ice cubes
  4. Sprinkle chopped nuts on top
  5. Serve chilled
